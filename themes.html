Priority Research Thematic Areas

Fourteen domains, one national priority

Aligned with the National Artificial Intelligence Policy 2025, AIDIRL prioritises problem-driven research across the following thematic domains. Several — most notably marine and aquaculture research and smart coastal-city infrastructure — carry particular significance for Gwadar and Balochistan.

Flagship

AI & NLP for Low-Resource & Regional Languages

Corpus construction, contextual embeddings, and named entity recognition for under-resourced Pakistani languages.

Includes benchmark development and instruction-tuning of large language models — AIDIRL's flagship research stream, anchored in work on Balochi.
Flagship

Multilingual & Multimodal Agentic AI Systems

Agentic AI applications spanning multiple Pakistani languages and modalities.

Includes automated public-service processes and multilingual, multimodal navigation assistance, such as TFAgent and NavAgent.
Flagship

Multilingual LLM Evaluation and Benchmarking

Culturally grounded, cross-lingual benchmarks assessing commonsense reasoning.

Measures general language-understanding performance gaps in state-of-the-art large language models, as in BGLUE and Global PIQA.
Governance

AI in Governance

E-government systems, digital public services, and data-driven decision platforms.

Focused on improving administrative efficiency, tax-fraud detection, and transparency in public institutions.
Healthcare

AI in Healthcare

Clinical decision support, telemedicine, disease surveillance, and AI-assisted diagnostics.

Targeting early detection and healthcare accessibility for underserved regions of Balochistan and beyond.
Agriculture

AI in Precision Agriculture

Crop-yield prediction and water-resource management using satellite imagery and IoT data.

Enhancing productivity in Balochistan's agricultural sector through remote sensing and field-level analytics.
Smart Cities

AI in Smart Cities & Urban Infrastructure

Intelligent urban planning, energy management, and smart-grid systems.

Predictive models for traffic and disaster resilience, supporting Gwadar's development as a smart coastal city.
Marine & Coastal

AI for Marine and Aquaculture

Monitoring coastal ecosystems and optimising fisheries and aquaculture operations.

Also covers automating port logistics and conducting environmental assessments to sustain marine resources — a priority for Gwadar's port economy.
Manufacturing

AI in Manufacturing Industry

Predictive maintenance, quality-control automation, and supply-chain optimisation.

Includes robotics integration to modernise industrial production processes.
Law

AI in Law and Judiciary

Legal document analysis, case-outcome prediction, and AI-assisted legal research.

Aimed at improving judicial efficiency and access to justice across the legal system.
Education

AI in Education

Personalised learning systems and intelligent tutoring.

Includes automated educational content generation adapted to Pakistan's classrooms.
Software

AI in the Software Industry

Code generation, software-testing automation, and AI-powered development tools.

Aims to accelerate local software innovation across Pakistan's growing tech sector.
FinTech

AI for FinTech

AI-driven credit scoring, fraud detection, and microfinance analytics.

Includes secure digital payment systems tailored to local communities and informal economies.
Energy & Climate

AI in Energy and Climate Change

Renewable-energy forecasting and smart-grid management.

AI-based modelling for climate adaptation and carbon monitoring in Balochistan's energy landscape.
Defense

AI in Defense and Strategic Organisations

Advanced data analytics, cybersecurity intelligence, and autonomous systems.

Contributes to strengthening national security and defense research capabilities.
