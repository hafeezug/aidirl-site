Publications

Publications affiliated with AIDIRL's flagship research programme, listed in reverse chronological order. The lab's early output centres on NLP for the Balochi language and multilingual evaluation of large language models, led by the Department's faculty.

2026

Global PIQA: Evaluating Physical Commonsense Reasoning Across 100+ Languages and Cultures

Tyler A. Chang, et al. — including Hafeez Ullah (co-author, Balochi language)

Submitted to NeurIPS · arXiv preprint arXiv:2510.24081

A massive participatory commonsense-reasoning benchmark built by 350+ researchers from 65+ countries, covering 141 language varieties. The Balochi contribution helped surface cross-lingual performance gaps in state-of-the-art LLMs.

arXiv PDF
2025

Balochi Named Entity Recognition Using Machine Learning Approach

Hafeez Ullah

In preparation

Introduces BNer, the first gold-standard Named Entity Recognition dataset for the Balochi language — 58,893 tokens across five domains. Evaluates CRF, SVM, and KNN approaches; SVM achieves the strongest F1-score using POS features and the BILOU tagging scheme.

2025

Word Embedding for Arabic Script Languages — A Survey

Hafeez Ullah

In preparation

A systematic review of over 100 research papers (2013–2025) tracking word-embedding techniques — from static embeddings to large language models — across Arabic-script languages, identifying resource gaps for languages like Balochi.
