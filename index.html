What AIDIRL Is

A dedicated home for the research frontier

While the Department's teaching laboratories serve the foundations of a computing education, AIDIRL is oriented squarely at original inquiry — the infrastructure, curated datasets, and collaborative environment needed to turn research insight into deployable, real-world solutions for Gwadar, Balochistan, and Pakistan.

Vision

A regional research hub

To be recognised as a centre of excellence that translates Pakistan's national AI priorities into locally grounded research and durable public datasets.

Mission

Problem-driven inquiry

To conduct applied research in AI, machine learning, and data science that is directly relevant to regional, national, and global development priorities.

Purpose

From insight to impact

To support faculty research, postgraduate and capstone supervision, dataset curation, and structured collaboration — all within a clear ethics and governance framework.

Focus

Fourteen thematic domains

From marine and coastal resource management to low-resource language AI — see the full set of research themes.
